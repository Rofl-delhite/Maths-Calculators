Multiplying Monomials Calculator

How to Use the Multiplying Monomials Calculator?

The procedure to use the multiplying monomials calculator is as follows:
Step 1: Enter the monomials in the respective input field
Step 2: Now click the button “Multiplying” to get the product
Step 3: Finally, the product of two monomials will be displayed in the new window

What is Meant by Multiplying Monomials?

In Mathematics, a monomial is a type of polynomial with only one term. It can have constants, variables, coefficients, and powers. If two monomials are multiplied the resultant will be a monomial. When two monomials are multiplied, first multiply the coefficients, and then multiply the variable terms. For example, 2x and 3b are the two monomials, the product of these two monomials will be 6ab.
