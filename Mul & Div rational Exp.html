How to Use the Multiplying and Dividing Rational Expressions Calculator?

The procedure to use the multiplying and dividing rational expressions calculator is as follows:
Step 1: Enter the rational expression in the input field
Step 2: Now click the button “Calculate” to get the solution
Step 3: Finally, the product or the quotient for the given rational expression will be displayed in the new window

What is Meant by Multiplying and Dividing Rational Expressions?

In Mathematics, a rational number is a number which is of the form p/q. Similarly, rational expression will be in the same form. It means it has a numerator and denominator, where the denominator should not be equal to 0. For the multiplication of the rational expression, first factor both the numerator and denominator and cancel out the possible factors and then multiply the remaining factors. To divide the rational expression, simplify flip the divisor expression and proceed with the multiplication process.
